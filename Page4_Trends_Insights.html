🪙 Toss Advantage 50.8% win rate for toss winners Toss impact is nearly negligible at a league level. Teams should focus on execution over conditions. Over-reliance on toss strategy is a cognitive bias, not a competitive edge.
☄️ Chasing Dominance 65.9% of captains choose to field after winning toss Chasing has become the dominant strategy. Setting a target creates psychological pressure on the second team — but teams have adapted. Batting first requires posting 180+ consistently.
🏆 Dynasty Patterns Gujarat Titans: 60.9% win rate in 3 seasons Short-tenure dominance suggests squad composition and captaincy matter more than franchise legacy. New teams can rapidly build winning cultures with smart auction strategy.
